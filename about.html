We are a contemporary Irish folk/Trad band from the village of Ardfinnan in County Tipperary, renowned for its broken bridge (among other things of course!) "Broken Bridges" blends Folk, Trad and contemporary music, creating a unique and distinctive sound. Catch some of our live performances for a memorable night of tunes, songs and plenty of craic!!!!
